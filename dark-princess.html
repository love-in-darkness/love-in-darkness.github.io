True Dark Princess

My dark princess is a queen among mortals, a ruler of hearts who reigns over the shadows with a captivating presence and enigmatic aura. She is a rose in the night, blooming with a beauty that transcends the darkness around her, and shining brightly in a world full of light. Her mysterious aura and piercing gaze have stolen my heart and captivated my soul, and I am grateful for every moment I spend in her presence. She is a true princess of darkness, embodying strength and resilience, and triumphing over every obstacle in her path.
